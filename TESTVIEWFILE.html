Mission

Pttr's mission is to fend for those who cant fend for them selves, to put a small roof over their heads and tails, and a bone under their bed. Pttr's mission is to help stray dogs find a suitable home, and loving owners. If the latter doesnt come to fruition we assist mans best friend in fighting for their lives by collecting donations from caring, and charitable individuals who care about innocent loving pets.

How Pttr Works

Pttr works with everyday individuals who are looking to adopt pets, and willing to give them a second shot at life. Pttr users are able to browse and eventually adopt pets. We access petfinder API and pull information reagrding stray animals who are up for adoption and match them with suitable owners. We also work with shelters who can sign up to Pttr and list animals in their shelter, the animals adoption history, and basic information about the type of pet it is. They are also enabled through Pttr torecieve donations by our users.
