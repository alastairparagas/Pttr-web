Profile Information

Why should you fill this out?

  • It is important for individuals and animal shelters to know a some of little bit about yourself before allowing you to adopt a pet of choice from their animal shelter.
  • This information is useful in order for nearby shelters that are selected based on your geolocation to select individuals who they see fit and appeal to the dogs up for adoption.
  • Animal Shelters also are signed up to Pttr and provide information about their shelter. Shelter information that is provided will tell you what animals they have up for adoption, if they accept donations.
